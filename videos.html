Videos

On this page you will find videos of different exercises that will help accelerate your learning. For some people, simply reading is not enough for them to retain the information they have learned. Some people learn easier by seeing exactly what they are supposed to do from someone that knows the technique. Use these videos to learn new exercises. Take advantage of pausing and rewinding the video to really get an understanding of what id being shown.
